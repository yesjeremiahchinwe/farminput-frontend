About FarmInput

Empowering African farmers through technology

Who We Are

FarmInput is a revolutionary agricultural technology platform dedicated to transforming how farmers manage their agricultural inputs across Africa. Founded in 2024, we recognized the challenges farmers face in tracking expenses, finding reliable suppliers and accessing competitive prices for essential farm inputs.

Our team combines deep agricultural expertise with cutting-edge technology to create solutions that address real farming challenges. We believe that every farmer deserves access to transparent pricing, quality inputs and the power of collective purchasing.

Today, FarmInput serves thousands of farmers across Nigeria and continues to expand throughout West Africa, helping agricultural communities thrive through better inputs management.

Our Mission

To empower smallholder farmers with transparent, accessible and affordable agricultural inputs solutions through innovative technology, enabling them to maximize productivity and profitability while building sustainable farming communities.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading agricultural inputs management platform transforming the way farmers access, purchase and manage farm inputs while fostering collaboration and knowledge sharing across farming communities.
