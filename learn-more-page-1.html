How FarmInput Works

Your complete guide to using FarmInput

Getting Started is Easy

Follow these simple steps to begin your journey

1

Access the platform

Visit our website and click “Sign In” to access the mobile-optimized application

2

Create your Account

Register with your phone number, verify with OTP, and complete your farmer profile

3

Complete Onboarding

Set up your farm details, preferences, and start exploring the platform features

4

Start Managing

Begin logging inputs, tracking spending, and joining buying groups to save money

Placing Orders Made Simple

Multiple ways to purchase your farm inputs

Individual Purchases

1

Browse Suppliers

Navigate to the Supplier Directory and browse verified suppliers in your region

2

Compare Prices

Use the Price Comparison tool to find the contact information provided

3

Contact Supplier

Call or message the supplier directly using the contact information provided

4

Log Your Purchase

After buying, log the input in the app to track to track your spending automatically

Group Purchases

Save up to 20%

1

Find or Create a Group

Navigate to the Supplier Directory and browse verified suppliers in your region

2

Join the Group

Request to join and chat with other members to coordinate the purchase

3

Confirm Your Quantity

Specify how much you need and see your discount price automatically

4

Complete Group Order

Once minimum quantity is met, the group order is placed with the supplier
